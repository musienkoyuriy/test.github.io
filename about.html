About DPLGuru

Sergio Retamal

After 22 years of executive supply chain and procurement experience, Sergio Retamal, the CEO of Global4PL & DPLGuru™, has become exceptionally well versed with the complex equations of the logistics industry. During his career stint with a big corporation, he naturally began to realize the scarcity of solutions to support In-Transit initiatives. He actively sought to launch a merge In-Transit initiative, but found there were no real solutions to support this type of program due to the large number of orders in a Just-in-Time (JIT)/ Merge In-Transit and in global scope. Recognizing the absence of a solution and encouraged by his diligence, Sergio set out to develop software that could support this operation, which eventually led to the inception of DPLGuru™.

DPLguru goal

DPLGuru™ addresses the current needs of global supply chain visibility, traceability, document retention, document creation and global trade compliance. There are so many regulations that software by itself is not enough– it takes the extra mile to review, cleanup and execute in the industry.
