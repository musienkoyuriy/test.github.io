FAQ

Does DPLGuru™ require complicated IT installation and deployment?

No. Our solution is a subscription-based cloud application, and our clients can start utilizing DPLGuru in a matter of hours! Our no-non-sense interface, is easy to understand, intuitive to use, and provides on-screen help for fields or topics where the user may require clarification. Additionally, our attractive subscription bundles include free consultation on-demand.

Who needs DPLGuru™?

Companies with significant in/out bound trade transactions, visibility of their supply chains, create and retain accurate record of related documentation, and are constantly monitoring and optimizing their logistics cost.

What does "compliance set-up" mean on the pricing table?

“Compliance set-up” is live consultation on legal and operational matters. The number of hours you receive in compliance set up is dependent on the package to which you subscribe.

How safe will my company’s data be?

The most secure encryption layers in the industry will safeguard your data. Your data will never be lost, thanks to deliberate encrypted file backup redundancy in the DPLGuru™ cloud platform.
